My Mobile Photography

As by name it is clear that Mobile photography is the art of photography using a smartphone or mobile device. Defined using the philosophy of 'the best camera is the one that's always with you', mobile photography is the future of the art form. Mobile is in our pocket so it is always accessible to capture a moment.
